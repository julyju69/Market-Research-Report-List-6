<p><strong>The Spectacle Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spacer Flange</li><li>Spade Flange</li><li>Spectacle Blind Flange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spectacle flanges are key components in piping systems, designed for isolation and maintenance. The market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Spacer Flange**: Serves as a bridging piece between two flanges, facilitating alignment without sealing.</p><p>2. **Spade Flange**: A solid disc used to block the pipeline flow, providing a reliable shut-off.</p><p>3. **Spectacle Blind Flange**: Combines the features of both spade and spacer flanges, allowing for quick isolation of sections in the system. Each type addresses specific operational needs in industrial applications.</p></p>
