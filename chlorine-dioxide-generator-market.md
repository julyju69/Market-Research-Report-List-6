<p><ul><li>Electrolytic Method</li><li>Chemical Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chlorine Dioxide Generator market is primarily categorized into two types: the electrolytic method and the chemical method. The electrolytic method generates chlorine dioxide through the electrolysis of sodium chloride solutions, offering precise control and lower operational costs. In contrast, the chemical method produces chlorine dioxide via the reaction of sodium chlorite with acids or chlorine, often favored for large-scale applications due to its simplicity and efficiency. Both methods cater to various industries, including water treatment, healthcare, and food processing.</p></p>
