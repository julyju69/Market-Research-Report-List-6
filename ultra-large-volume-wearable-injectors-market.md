<p><p>The ultra-large volume wearable injectors market comprises two main types: on-body and off-body injectors. On-body injectors are designed to be worn directly on the skin, facilitating the administration of larger volumes of medication over extended periods. In contrast, off-body injectors are separate devices that are not attached to the user's body but can deliver medication via a connection to a subcutaneous infusion system. Both types aim to enhance patient comfort and adherence while enabling efficient drug delivery for chronic conditions.</p></p>
