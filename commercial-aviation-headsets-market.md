<p><strong>The Commercial Aviation Headsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Noise Reduction Headsets</li><li>Active Noise Reduction Headsets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial aviation headsets market can be categorized into two main types: passive noise reduction headsets and active noise reduction headsets. Passive noise reduction headsets rely on physical barriers to block external sounds, typically using cushioned ear cups to isolate the user from ambient noise. In contrast, active noise reduction headsets utilize advanced technology, including microphones and speakers, to detect and cancel out unwanted sounds, providing a quieter environment. Both types enhance communication and comfort for pilots and crew during flight.</p></p>
