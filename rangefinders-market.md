<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Rangefinder</li><li>Acoustic Rangefinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rangefinder market is segmented primarily into photoelectric and acoustic rangefinders. Photoelectric rangefinders utilize light waves, typically laser, to measure distances with high precision and are widely used in surveying, construction, and outdoor activities. Acoustic rangefinders, on the other hand, employ sound waves to compute distances, often used in underwater applications and by certain wildlife researchers. Both types cater to specific needs, promoting advancements and innovations in measurement technologies across various industries.</p></p>
