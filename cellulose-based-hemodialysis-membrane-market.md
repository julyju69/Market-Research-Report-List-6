<p><p>The cellulose-based hemodialysis membrane market is primarily divided into two types: regenerated cellulose membrane and modified cellulose membrane. Regenerated cellulose membranes are derived from natural cellulose sources, offering biocompatibility and effective solute clearance. In contrast, modified cellulose membranes incorporate additives or chemical treatments to enhance performance characteristics, such as reduced protein adsorption and improved permeability. Both types are essential for optimizing hemodialysis procedures, ensuring efficient waste removal and patient safety during renal therapies.</p></p>
