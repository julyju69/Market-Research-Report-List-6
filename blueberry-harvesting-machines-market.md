<p><ul><li>Half-row Harvesting Machines</li><li>Full-row Harvesting Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blueberry harvesting machines can be categorized into two main types: half-row and full-row harvesting machines. Half-row machines are designed to pick berries from only one side of the bush, making them suitable for narrower fields and providing gentler handling of the fruit. In contrast, full-row machines harvest berries from both sides simultaneously, increasing efficiency and productivity in wider fields. The choice between these types depends on the layout of the blueberry farm and the desired harvesting speed.</p></p>
