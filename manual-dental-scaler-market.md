<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Manual Dental Scaler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sickle</li><li>Curette</li><li>File</li><li>Hoe</li><li>Chisel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual dental scaler market comprises various types designed for different scaling tasks. Sickle scalers feature a pointed, curved working end for removing supra-gingival plaque. Curettes, with their rounded ends, are ideal for both supra- and sub-gingival cleaning. Files are robust instruments suited for heavy calculus removal, while hoes feature a flat blade for effective scaling of tooth surfaces. Lastly, chisels are used for specific tasks involving tooth contouring and are effective in interproximal areas, making them essential in dental hygiene practices.</p></p>
