<p><p>The sedan vehicle thermostat market is primarily divided into two main types: insert thermostats and housing thermostats. Insert thermostats are compact components that regulate engine temperature by controlling coolant flow, fitting directly into the engine block. In contrast, housing thermostats consist of a larger assembly that includes the thermostat and housing, serving as a more integrated solution for temperature management. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining optimal engine performance and fuel efficiency by preventing overheating and ensuring the engine operates within the desired temperature range.</p></p>
