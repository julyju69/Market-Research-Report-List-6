<p><p>The Laser Vision Sensor market comprises two main types: Monocular Vision Sensors and Binocular Vision Sensors. Monocular Vision Sensors utilize a single camera to capture images, enabling depth perception through algorithms and data processing. They are generally compact and cost-effective. In contrast, Binocular Vision Sensors employ two cameras, mimicking human eyesight, to provide enhanced depth perception and accuracy in 3D imaging. This makes them ideal for complex environments and applications requiring precise measurements, such as robotics and automation.</p></p>
