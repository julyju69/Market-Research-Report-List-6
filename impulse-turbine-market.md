<p><p>An impulse turbine is a type of turbine that converts the kinetic energy of a fluid into mechanical energy through the use of blades. In contrast to reaction turbines, which use both pressure and flow to generate power, impulse turbines operate solely on the energy derived from high-velocity jets of fluid striking the turbine blades. This design makes them particularly effective in applications with high heads and low flow rates, such as hydroelectric plants and certain industrial processes.</p><p>The Impulse Turbine Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for renewable energy sources and advancements in turbine technology. The focus on sustainability and climate change mitigation has led to greater investments in hydroelectric projects globally.
