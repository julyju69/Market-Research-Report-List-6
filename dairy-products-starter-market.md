<p><p>The dairy products starter market is categorized into three main types: mesophilic, thermophilic, and probiotics. Mesophilic starters thrive at moderate temperatures, commonly used in yogurt and soft cheeses, enhancing flavor and texture. Thermophilic starters operate at higher temperatures, ideal for making hard cheeses and yogurt, providing distinct tastes and extended shelf life. Probiotics are beneficial microorganisms promoting gut health and are increasingly incorporated into dairy products, offering added health benefits and driving consumer demand for functional foods.</p></p>
