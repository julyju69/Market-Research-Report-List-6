<p><p>Sealed Maintenance Free (SMF) batteries are designed to require no liquid maintenance, making them highly convenient for various applications. The two primary types are Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) batteries, which use fiberglass mats to hold electrolyte, offering high discharge rates and durability, and Gel batteries, which contain a silica-based gel that solidifies the electrolyte, providing better resistance to vibration and temperature extremes. Both types are widely used in automotive, renewable energy, and backup power systems due to their reliability and performance.</p></p>
