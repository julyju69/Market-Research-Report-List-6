<p><p>The MLCC (Multi-layer Ceramic Capacitor) market comprises two primary types of paste: inner electrode paste and outer electrode paste. Inner electrode paste is used to form the internal conductive layers within the ceramic capacitor, ensuring efficient electrical performance and reliability. In contrast, outer electrode paste serves as the external conductive layer, facilitating connections to the circuit. Both types are essential for enhancing the overall functionality and durability of MLCCs, which are crucial components in modern electronic devices.</p></p>
