<p><p>The flow cytometry reagents market is categorized into three main types: single-color reagents, two-color reagents, and multicolor reagents. Single-color reagents are designed to label one specific target, allowing for straightforward analysis. Two-color reagents provide enhanced resolution by enabling the simultaneous detection of two different targets, improving specificity. Multicolor reagents maximize information by allowing the simultaneous detection of multiple targets, facilitating complex analyses in various applications such as immunology, cancer research, and cell biology.</p></p>
