<p><p>Collaboration applications are categorized into two primary market types: on-premises and on-demand. On-premises applications are installed and managed within an organization's own IT infrastructure, offering greater control and security but requiring significant resources for maintenance. In contrast, on-demand applications, often referred to as cloud-based solutions, are hosted by service providers and accessed via the internet. This model fosters scalability and ease of use, allowing organizations to quickly adapt to changing needs without heavy upfront costs.</p></p>
