<p><p>The PVDC barrier material market consists primarily of two types: PVDC resins and PVDC latex. PVDC resins are solid polymers known for their excellent barrier properties against gases and moisture, making them ideal for packaging applications in food and pharmaceuticals. On the other hand, PVDC latex is a water-based dispersion that offers flexible coating solutions for various substrates, enhancing barrier characteristics while being environmentally friendly. Together, they serve diverse industries by providing effective preservation and protection solutions.</p></p>
