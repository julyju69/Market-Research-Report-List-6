<p><p>The medical tunable laser market features three main types of control technologies. Current control technology adjusts the laser's output by modulating electrical current, ensuring precise wavelength tuning. Temperature control technology manages the laser's operational temperature to stabilize performance and enhance output consistency. Mechanical control technology utilizes physical adjustments, such as moving components, to achieve desired wavelengths. Together, these technologies enhance the versatility and functionality of tunable lasers in medical applications, including diagnostics and therapeutic procedures.</p></p>
