<p><p>The cylindrical power battery market primarily features two types: cylindrical ternary batteries and cylindrical lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries. Cylindrical ternary batteries, utilizing nickel, cobalt, and manganese, offer high energy density and are commonly used in electric vehicles and portable electronics. In contrast, cylindrical lithium iron phosphate batteries prioritize safety and thermal stability, making them ideal for applications where durability is essential, such as renewable energy storage and electric buses. Both types play crucial roles in the growing battery market.</p></p>
