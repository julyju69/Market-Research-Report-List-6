<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM) is a high-performance thermoplastic polymer known for its rigidity, low friction, and excellent dimensional stability. The POM market is segmented into two main types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-POM is characterized by its uniform molecular structure, offering superior mechanical strength and wear resistance, making it suitable for engineering applications. Co-POM, on the other hand, incorporates different comonomers to enhance flexibility and processability, making it ideal for applications requiring more complex material properties.</p></p>
