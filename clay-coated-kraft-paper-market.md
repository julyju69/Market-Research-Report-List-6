<p><p>Clay Coated Kraft Paper is categorized into two main types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided clay coated paper features a smooth, glossy finish on one side, ideal for high-quality printing and packaging where aesthetics are crucial. In contrast, double-sided clay coated paper has a coated surface on both sides, offering versatility for applications that require printability and durability on either side. Both types are widely used in packaging, labels, and promotional materials, catering to various industry needs.</p></p>
