<p><p>The epitaxial reactor market is primarily segmented into three types: Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition (MOCVD), Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE), and other methods such as Vapor Phase Epitaxy (VPE), Liquid Phase Epitaxy (LPE), and Solid Phase Epitaxy (SPE). MOCVD is favored for producing compound semiconductors, especially in LEDs and solar cells, while MBE offers precise control for high-purity layers. VPE, LPE, and SPE are used for specialized applications, each offering unique advantages in material quality and growth techniques, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
