<p><p>The exhaust flange gasket market features several types, each serving specific applications. Tapered rings offer a tight seal by adapting to various flange dimensions. Crush rings create a secure seal through compression, ideal for high-temperature environments. Steel rings provide durability and longevity, suitable for heavy machinery and high-performance engines. Steel mesh rings deliver flexibility and resistance to deformation, ensuring effective sealing under fluctuating pressures. Each type is designed to enhance performance and reliability in exhaust systems.</p></p>
