<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive dash camera market is primarily divided into two types: single channel and multi-channel systems. Single channel dash cameras record video from one lens, typically capturing the front view of the vehicle. They are simpler and often more affordable, catering to basic surveillance needs. In contrast, multi-channel dash cameras include multiple lenses—usually covering the front, rear, and interior—providing comprehensive coverage and enhanced security. Multi-channel systems are ideal for those seeking detailed incident documentation and monitoring from various angles.</p></p>
