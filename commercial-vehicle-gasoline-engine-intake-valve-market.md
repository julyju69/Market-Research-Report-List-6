<p><p>The commercial vehicle gasoline engine intake valve market comprises three key types: mono metallic valves, bimetallic valves, and hollow valves. Mono metallic valves are made from a single metal, providing durability and corrosion resistance. Bimetallic valves combine two different metals to optimize thermal and mechanical properties, enhancing performance under extreme conditions. Hollow valves, designed with a hollow structure, reduce weight and improve airflow, contributing to better engine efficiency. Each type caters to varying performance needs and engine designs in the commercial vehicle sector.</p></p>
