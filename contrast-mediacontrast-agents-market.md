<p><p>The contrast media market includes iodinated, gadolinium-based, and barium-based agents, each serving distinct imaging techniques. Iodinated contrast agents are primarily used in X-ray and CT scans to enhance vascular and organ visualization. Gadolinium-based agents are utilized in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) to improve soft tissue contrast. Barium-based agents are essential for gastrointestinal imaging, providing clarity in X-ray procedures. Each type is specifically formulated to optimize imaging quality while minimizing patient risk.</p></p>
