<p><p>The piezoceramic components for the automotive market can be categorized into single plate and multilayer piezo ceramics. Single plate piezo ceramics are typically used for applications requiring simple actuation or sensing, providing basic functionality and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, multilayer piezo ceramics consist of several layers stacked together, enabling higher performance, increased sensitivity, and greater displacement capabilities. These advanced components are essential for applications like fuel injection systems, vibration control, and adaptive seating, enhancing vehicle performance and comfort.</p></p>
