<p><p>The Terahertz Nondestructive Testing (NDT) market comprises various methods used to assess materials and structures without causing damage. Eddy Current Testing utilizes electromagnetic induction to detect surface and near-surface flaws. Ultrasonic Testing employs high-frequency sound waves to identify internal defects. Radiography uses X-rays or gamma rays for imaging internal structures. Sonic Testing measures vibrations to evaluate material integrity. Magnetic Particle Testing identifies surface anomalies in ferromagnetic materials. Additional methods, categorized as "Others," encompass various advanced techniques enhancing inspection capabilities.</p></p>
