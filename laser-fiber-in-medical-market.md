<p><p>In the medical market, laser fibers are categorized into single-use and reusable types. Single-use medical laser fibers are designed for one-time procedures, ensuring optimal sterility and minimizing cross-contamination risks. They offer convenience and eliminate the need for cleaning and maintenance, making them suitable for high-turnover settings. Reusable medical laser fibers, on the other hand, are designed for multiple uses after proper sterilization. They are cost-effective over time but require careful handling and rigorous cleaning protocols between uses to ensure patient safety and performance.</p></p>
