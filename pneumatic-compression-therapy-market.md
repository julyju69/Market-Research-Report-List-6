<p><p>The pneumatic compression therapy market includes various types of devices designed to enhance blood circulation and treat conditions such as lymphedema and venous insufficiency. Pneumatic compression sleeves provide external pressure to limbs, while segmented pneumatic compression pumps offer targeted inflation across multiple chambers. Programmable pneumatic compression pumps allow for customized treatment cycles, whereas non-programmable versions provide fixed settings. Non-segmented pneumatic compression pumps deliver consistent pressure but lack chamber divisions, catering to diverse patient needs and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
