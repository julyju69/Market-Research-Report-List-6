<p><p>The Rape Honey market consists of three main types: extracted honey, pressed honey, and comb honey. Extracted honey is processed and filtered, resulting in a clear, liquid form, often favored for its smooth texture and ease of use. Pressed honey is obtained by crushing honeycomb and allowing the liquid to flow out, retaining some pollen and wax. Comb honey is sold in its natural state within the wax cells, appealing for its rustic presentation and unique flavor profile. Each type offers distinct characteristics catering to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
