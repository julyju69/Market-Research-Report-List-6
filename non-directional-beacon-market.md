<p><ul><li>Medium Frequency (MF) Non-directional Beacon</li><li>Low Frequency (LF) Non-directional Beacon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-directional Beacon (NDB) market encompasses two primary types: Medium Frequency (MF) and Low Frequency (LF) NDBs. MF NDBs typically operate in the frequency range of 300 kHz to 3 MHz, providing reliable navigation signals for aircraft and marine vessels. LF NDBs, operating below 300 kHz, are utilized for long-range navigation, particularly in adverse weather conditions. Both types are essential for enhancing safety in aviation and maritime operations by enabling accurate positioning and navigation, while fostering advancements in communication technologies.</p></p>
