<p><ul><li>Non Homogeneous</li><li>Homogeneous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rhodium Carbon Catalyst market can be segmented into non-homogeneous and homogeneous types based on their catalytic systems. Non-homogeneous catalysts consist of solid rhodium dispersed in a liquid medium, allowing for easier recovery after reactions. In contrast, homogeneous catalysts involve rhodium ions uniformly dissolved within the reaction mixture, which enhances selectivity but complicates separation. Each type serves specific applications, with non-homogeneous catalysts often favored for industrial processes, while homogeneous catalysts excel in fine chemical synthesis due to their efficiency.</p></p>
