<p><ul><li>Vacuum Conveying System</li><li>Non-vacuum Conveying System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powder conveying system market is categorized into two main types: vacuum and non-vacuum conveying systems. Vacuum conveying systems use suction to transport powders through pipelines, ensuring minimal dust emission and material spillage, making them ideal for sensitive environments. In contrast, non-vacuum conveying systems rely on mechanical means, such as screw conveyors or pneumatic blowers, to move powders. Each system has distinct advantages, with vacuum systems offering enhanced safety and cleanliness while non-vacuum systems provide cost-effective solutions for various applications.</p></p>
