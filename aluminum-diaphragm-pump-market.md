<p><strong>The Aluminum Diaphragm Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting</li><li>Double Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum diaphragm pump market features two primary types: single acting and double acting. Single acting pumps utilize one diaphragm, pumping fluid on every stroke, making them suitable for low-flow applications. In contrast, double acting pumps employ two diaphragms, allowing fluid to move during both the forward and return strokes, resulting in higher efficiency and continuous flow. These types cater to various industries, including chemical processing, water treatment, and pharmaceuticals, offering distinct advantages based on application needs.</p></p>
