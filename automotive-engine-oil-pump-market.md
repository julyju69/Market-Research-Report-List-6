<p><ul><li>Mechanical Oil Pump</li><li>Electric Oil Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine oil pump market comprises two primary types: mechanical oil pumps and electric oil pumps. Mechanical oil pumps, often driven by the engine's movement, rely on gears or rotors to circulate oil, ensuring efficient lubrication and cooling. In contrast, electric oil pumps utilize electric motors to provide more controlled and variable oil flow, enhancing efficiency and performance, especially in modern vehicles with advanced engine management systems. Both types play vital roles in maintaining engine health and performance.</p></p>
