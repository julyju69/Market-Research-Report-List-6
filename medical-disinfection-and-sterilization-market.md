<p><p>The medical disinfection and sterilization market encompasses various methods, primarily focusing on steam sterilizers and ethylene oxide sterilizers. Steam sterilizers use high-pressure steam to eliminate microorganisms and are widely favored for their efficiency and fast cycle times. Ethylene oxide sterilizers, on the other hand, employ ethylene oxide gas to sterilize heat-sensitive medical equipment, offering a thorough yet slower process. Both methods are essential in healthcare settings to ensure the safety and hygiene of medical instruments and reduce infection risks.</p></p>
