<p><ul><li>Flexible Foam</li><li>Rigid Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The packaging foam market is primarily divided into two types: flexible foam and rigid foam. Flexible foam is lightweight, compressible, and often used for protective cushioning in various packaging applications, such as electronics and fragile items. It provides excellent shock absorption and is widely sought for its versatility. Rigid foam, on the other hand, offers superior structural support and insulation, making it ideal for heavy or temperature-sensitive products. Both types enhance product safety during transit and storage, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
