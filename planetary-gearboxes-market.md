<p><ul><li>Linear Planetary Gearboxes</li><li>Right Angle Planetary Gearboxes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The planetary gearboxes market is segmented into linear and right-angle types. Linear planetary gearboxes deliver motion primarily in a straight line, making them suitable for applications requiring high precision and torque. Conversely, right-angle planetary gearboxes provide a 90-degree output shaft orientation, making them ideal for space-constrained environments and applications needing direction changes. Both types enhance efficiency, reduce backlash, and offer compact designs, catering to diverse industries such as robotics, automation, and automotive systems.</p></p>
